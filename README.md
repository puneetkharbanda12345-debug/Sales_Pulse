# Sales_Pulse
SalesPulse – Interactive Sales Analytics Dashboard
  Project Overview

SalesPulse is a Power BI dashboard created to help businesses analyze sales performance through interactive and easy-to-understand visual reports. The dashboard converts raw sales records into meaningful insights by organizing data into clear visualizations that highlight revenue trends, customer behavior, product performance, and overall business growth.

The project follows a complete Business Intelligence workflow—from importing and cleaning data to building relationships, creating DAX measures, and designing an interactive dashboard. It enables users to explore sales information from multiple perspectives, identify business opportunities, and make informed decisions using real-time insights.

This project reflects practical experience in data analytics, dashboard development, and business reporting while demonstrating the effective use of Power BI for solving real-world business problems.

  Project Objective

The primary goal of this project was to build a scalable and interactive Business Intelligence solution capable of transforming raw sales data into valuable business insights. By combining data modeling, DAX calculations, and intuitive visualizations, the dashboard provides decision-makers with a clear understanding of sales performance, customer trends, and product effectiveness. This project also serves as a demonstration of practical Power BI skills, analytical thinking, and the ability to develop business-focused reporting solutions.
